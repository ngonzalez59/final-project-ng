Getting Your Life Together (GYLT)

If you ever feel like your life is spiraling out of control and you have no way to organize yourself, this tool will surely help in getting your life together (no pun intended). All you have to do is write the task(s) that you wish to complete. You can add as many, or a few as you would like--though I don't recommend going overboard as you will most likely overwhelm yourself. This is merely a tool that is meant to help you feel like you have more structure in your life than you may actually have.

Once you have completed a task you can check it, or you can cross it out completely. The satisfaction of having fewer items will surely be worth the effort. Be sure to rid yourself of any GYLT (pun intended) as you use this tool to, hopefully, stop procrastinating.
